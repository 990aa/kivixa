Kivixa is a privacy-first cross-platform productivity workspace for notes, sketching, planning, and local AI assistance.

Features:

- Rich markdown and text editing for fast note-taking

- Digital canvas for handwriting, drawing, and mixed media notes

- On-device AI with multi-model MCP workflows (no cloud required)

- Knowledge graph view to connect ideas and topics visually

- PDF reading and annotation inside the same workspace

- Focus tools including Pomodoro and custom productivity timers

- High-performance math tools powered by a Rust backend
